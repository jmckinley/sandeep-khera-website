Frequently Asked Questions

High-performance coaching is a structured, results-driven approach designed specifically for high achievers. Unlike general life coaching, it focuses on developing specific skills, habits, and mindset shifts that lead to measurable improvements in both personal and professional areas. Sandeep Khera's approach combines behavioral science, neuroscience, and proven performance strategies.

Sandeep Khera brings over 30 years of C-level executive experience from organizations like Credit Suisse, EY, and Deloitte. This real-world experience, combined with his certification as a high-performance coach, provides unique insights that most coaches cannot offer. His approach is holistic, addressing mindset, health, wealth, relationships, business, leadership, and happiness.

The discovery call is a free 30-minute conversation where we discuss your current situation, goals, and challenges. It's an opportunity for you to ask questions and for us to determine if coaching is the right fit for your needs. There's no pressure or obligation—it's simply a conversation to explore possibilities.

Yes, all coaching sessions can be conducted virtually via Zoom or similar platforms. This allows Sandeep Khera to work with clients globally, regardless of location. Virtual sessions are just as effective as in-person meetings and offer greater flexibility for busy professionals.
