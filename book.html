There Is No Right or Wrong, There Are Only Lessons

In this transformative book, Sandeep Khera shares pivotal moments and insights from his personal and professional journey spanning over three decades. Each chapter reveals a lesson learned through experience—from boardroom victories to personal challenges—offering readers practical wisdom they can apply to their own lives.

About the Author

Sandeep Khera

Sandeep Khera is a high-performance coach, C-level executive, and entrepreneur with over 30 years of international business experience. His career spans leadership roles at Credit Suisse, Standard Chartered Bank, Ernst & Young, Commonwealth Bank of Australia, and Deloitte Consulting.

Named among the "Top 50 Most Influential Executives" and "100 Most Influential Coaches in Asia," Sandeep now dedicates his life to empowering others through coaching, speaking, and writing. His book draws from real experiences—both triumphs and failures—to provide authentic, actionable wisdom.
