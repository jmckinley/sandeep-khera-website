About

Sandeep Khera

High-Performance Coach, Author & Entrepreneur

The Story

A Catalyst for Profound Transformation

Sandeep Khera is not just a coach; he is a catalyst for profound transformation. With over 30 years of international business experience as a C-level Executive, Author, and Entrepreneur, Sandeep's journey was fueled by grit, curiosity, and an unshakable desire to grow.

Raised in a middle-class family, Sandeep's early interest in human psychology and behavioral science shaped his life's mission. His career spans major global organizations including Credit Suisse, Standard Chartered Bank, Ernst & Young, Commonwealth Bank of Australia, and Deloitte Consulting.

This extensive experience provides him deep understanding of effective executive leadership. Currently serving as CEO and Managing Partner of Fexle APAC, Sandeep has been recognized among the "Top 50 Most Influential Executives" and "100 Most Influential Coaches in Asia."

His mission centers on empowering professionals to live with purpose, performance, and passion. He helps others dream bigger, reach higher, and lead with purpose.

The System

High-Performance Coaching Framework

A high-performance coach helps individuals unlock their full potential by fostering a growth mindset, enhancing mental toughness, and developing resilience to overcome challenges.

Sandeep Khera's coaching system is built on decades of research and real-world application. It focuses on seven key pillars:

  • Mindset: Reprogram limiting beliefs and develop unstoppable confidence
  • Health: Optimize physical and mental well-being for peak performance
  • Wealth: Create strategic financial growth and lasting abundance
  • Relationships: Build meaningful connections that support your growth
  • Business: Lead with influence and achieve excellence
  • Leadership: Inspire teams and create lasting impact
  • Happiness: Align with your purpose and live with fulfillment

This comprehensive approach ensures transformation across all areas of life, not just isolated improvements.

The Science

Evidence-Based Transformation

High-performance coaching differs from life coaching in its structured, results-driven approach designed specifically for high achievers. The methodology is rooted in:

  • Behavioral Science: Understanding how habits form and how to create lasting change
  • Neuroscience: Leveraging brain plasticity to rewire thought patterns
  • Positive Psychology: Building on strengths rather than just fixing weaknesses
  • Performance Psychology: Techniques used by elite athletes and executives

Every coaching session is designed with specific outcomes in mind, using proven frameworks that have helped thousands of professionals achieve breakthrough results.

The combination of Sandeep's corporate experience and scientific approach creates a unique coaching methodology that delivers measurable, sustainable results.
